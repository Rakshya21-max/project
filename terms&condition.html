1. Introduction

Welcome to our Dog Adoption Platform. These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern your use of our website, mobile application, and all services related to dog adoption. By accessing and using our platform, you agree to abide by these terms. If you do not agree with any part of these terms, please do not use our services.

We are committed to finding loving homes for rescue dogs and ensuring the welfare and safety of all animals in our care.

2. Eligibility and User Accounts

2.1 Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to use our platform and participate in the adoption process.

2.2 Account Registration

To adopt a dog, you must create an account and provide accurate, complete, and truthful information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password and account information.

2.3 Account Suspension

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account if you violate these terms or engage in any conduct that is harmful to animals or other users.

3. Adoption Eligibility and Requirements

3.1 Pre-Adoption Screening

All adopters must complete a comprehensive application form and undergo a screening process. This includes:

  • Providing personal and household information
  • Demonstrating commitment to animal care and welfare
  • Proof of residence and identity verification
  • References from previous pets or veterinary records
  • Home visit (may be required for certain cases)

3.2 Adoption Criteria

Applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Be able to provide a safe, suitable living environment for the dog
  • Have the financial means to provide proper care, including food, medical treatment, and emergency veterinary care
  • Be able to commit to the dog for its entire lifetime (typically 10-15 years)
  • Have a fenced yard or alternative exercise arrangements (where applicable)
  • No history of animal abuse or neglect
  • Comply with local pet ownership laws and regulations

3.3 Right of Refusal

We reserve the right to deny any adoption application at our sole discretion if we believe it is not in the best interest of the dog.

4. Adoption Agreement

4.1 Binding Agreement

Once an adoption is approved, the adopter agrees to sign a legally binding adoption agreement that includes:

  • Commitment to provide proper care, food, shelter, and medical treatment
  • Agreement to not abandon, abuse, or neglect the dog
  • Right of the platform/organization to reclaim the dog if abuse or neglect is discovered
  • Agreement to notify us if circumstances change and the dog can no longer be cared for
  • Commitment to return the dog to us if adoption must be dissolved

4.2 Adoption Fee

An adoption fee is required to cover veterinary care, vaccinations, and administrative costs. This fee is non-refundable once the adoption is finalized.

5. Care Responsibilities

5.1 Essential Care

Adopters agree to provide:

  • Nutritious food and fresh water daily
  • Adequate shelter and safe living environment
  • Regular exercise and mental stimulation
  • Preventive veterinary care (vaccinations, check-ups)
  • Treatment for any illness or injury promptly
  • Training and socialization as needed
  • Love, attention, and companionship

5.2 Veterinary Care

The adopter is fully responsible for all veterinary expenses. We strongly recommend maintaining pet health insurance and budgeting for emergency veterinary care.

5.3 Legal Compliance

Adopters must comply with all local, state, and federal laws regarding pet ownership, including licensing, vaccinations, and any breed restrictions.

6. Dog Health and Warranty

6.1 Health Status

All dogs are examined by a veterinarian before adoption. Information about the dog's health, medical history, and any known behavioral issues will be provided to the adopter.

6.2 Limited Health Guarantee

We provide a limited health guarantee of 14 days from the adoption date. If the dog is diagnosed with a major illness or condition not previously disclosed, contact us immediately for assistance or a replacement.

6.3 No Warranty

After the 14-day period, the adopter assumes full responsibility for the dog's health and wellbeing.

7. Return Policy

7.1 Return Conditions

If the adoption is not working out, the adopter must contact us immediately. Dogs may be returned within 30 days for a full refund of the adoption fee, provided:

  • The dog shows no signs of abuse or neglect
  • The dog is in the same condition as when adopted
  • The dog has not bitten or injured anyone

7.2 Return Procedure

Contact our adoption coordinator before returning the dog to arrange pickup or drop-off.

7.3 Non-Refundable Items

Medical records, certifications, and any purchased items are non-refundable.

8. Platform Content and Accuracy

8.1 Dog Profiles

While we strive to provide accurate information about each dog, we make no guarantees regarding personality, temperament, or behavior. Each dog is an individual and may behave differently in a new home.

8.2 Photographs and Descriptions

Photographs and descriptions are for reference only. Actual appearance and behavior may vary. We recommend meeting the dog in person before adopting.

8.3 User-Generated Content

Users agree not to post misleading, false, or defamatory information on our platform.

9. Privacy and Data Protection

Your personal information will be collected and used solely for the adoption process and to maintain contact regarding your adopted dog. We will not share your information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

For complete details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

10. Liability and Indemnification

10.1 Limitation of Liability

We are not responsible for any injuries, damages, or losses caused by an adopted dog. The adopter assumes full responsibility for the dog's actions.

10.2 Indemnification

Adopters agree to indemnify and hold harmless the platform, its staff, volunteers, and partners from any claims, damages, or legal actions arising from the adoption or the dog's behavior.

10.3 Third-Party Liability

We strongly recommend obtaining pet liability insurance to protect yourself and others.

11. Behavioral Issues and Disclosure

11.1 Known Issues

We disclose any known behavioral issues, fears, or health concerns about each dog. Adopters acknowledge receipt of this information.

11.2 Undisclosed Issues

If serious behavioral issues develop that were not disclosed, contact us within 30 days for guidance and support.

11.3 Dangerous Behavior

If an adopted dog shows signs of dangerous behavior that poses a risk to people or other animals, the adopter must notify us immediately and return the dog if necessary.

12. Prohibited Activities

Adopters agree not to:

  • Abuse, neglect, or mistreat the dog in any way
  • Use the dog for breeding, fighting, or any illegal purpose
  • Chain or confine the dog permanently without proper veterinary care
  • Surrender the dog to a shelter or animal control without notifying us first
  • Resell or transfer the dog to another person without our consent
  • Use the dog for commercial purposes without written permission
  • Fail to provide required vaccinations or preventive care

Violation of these terms may result in legal action, criminal charges, and/or reclaim of the dog.

13. Post-Adoption Support

We provide ongoing support to adopters, including:

  • Training and behavioral advice
  • Veterinary referrals and guidance
  • Assistance with any adjustment issues
  • Free resources and educational materials
  • Update requests and follow-up check-ins
