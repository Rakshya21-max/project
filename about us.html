Our Mission

Creating a World Where Every Dog Has a Home

Founded in 2016, RescueTails emerged from a simple belief: every street dog deserves a chance at a better life. What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into a comprehensive rescue organization that has transformed the lives of thousands of dogs and families.

We work tirelessly to rescue abandoned and stray dogs from the streets, provide medical care, rehabilitation, and rehoming services‚Äîhelping them find loving forever homes where they can thrive.
